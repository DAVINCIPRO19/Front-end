EDUCATION SYSTEM AND TYPES OF PROGRAMS


The education system of Spain is built on European standards and integrates new programs aimed at both local students and foreign students. More and more state universities provide training programs for undergraduate and graduate programs, which greatly facilitates the way for international students to obtain the desired degree. It is important to note that state universities teach in Spanish, private universities in Spanish and English.
SECONDARY EDUCATION
In Spanish schools, students study for 12 years. After grade 9, active preparation for the final exam begins - SELECTIVIDAD. The exam consists of required subjects: Spanish, Spanish history, English; and subjects of choice - they can be from two to four, it is important to choose a subject that will be of value to the admissions committee of the university upon admission to a particular specialty.Foreign students have privileges when passing this exam, because the grades of our certificate also bring their extra points. Foreign students undergo annual training for Selictividad, which includes 4-5 months of studying the Spanish language and direct preparation for the exam itself, after which they submit applications to universities.
