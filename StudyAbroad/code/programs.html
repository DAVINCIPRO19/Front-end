LANGUAGE CLASSES ABROAD

Language courses abroad is the best way to learn a language. STUDY ABROAD provides assistance in enrolling in language programs around the world and provides full support from choosing a country to completing your course. The widest selection of different types of language programs and up-to-date information on discounts and current promotions of international language schools.Choose your type of program and order a selection of options and an individual cost calculation for selected areas.

COURSES FOR ADULTS 16+


Language courses for adult students are focused on language training, passing international exams and obtaining additional certificates related to language learning and basic business skills.

Language courses abroad are the best way to learn a language from any level! Full immersion easily and naturally eliminates the language barrier and allows you to learn the language you are learning directly in a living communication environment. The program is available from 16 years and there is no limit! If you are ready to study, then international language schools are ready to help you!


PROGRAM FACTS


Minimum age 16 years
Minimum booking period is 1 week
Selection of accommodation and assistance in adapting to the language
Admission of students with a 0 level of language proficiency
Availability of 30+ and 50+ programs

PREPARATION FOR ARRIVAL 16+


Language training programs for applicants, during which the student tightens the English language and studies the educational system of the country where he plans to study (requirements and format of assignment). The final goal of the program: to confirm the level of language sufficient for transition to a university.

Language preparation for admission - PATHWAY - the surest way to raise the level of language for further admission to an educational institution. Such programs include not only language courses, but also the study of academic skills for a particular education system. This will prepare any student for a successful study!


FACTS ABOUT PREPARATION PROGRAMS (PATHWAY)


Minimum age 16 years
Duration of study from 12 weeks to 2 years
The ability to do without IELTS in some countries
Adaptation of the future student to the education system
The opportunity to prepare for higher-ranking universities

HOLIDAYS FOR CHILDREN 12+


The best way to spend your vacation is to go abroad to learn a foreign language! You can go in a group or individually. Children's language camp programs include round-the-clock custody, 20 lessons per week, room and board, as well as sightseeing and entertainment programs.

Language courses abroad for children and adolescents are an ideal option for spending fun and interesting vacations with benefit. This type of language courses differs from standard ones in that students are under guardianship 24/7 and the price includes an entertainment and sightseeing program for the entire period of study.


PROGRAM FACTS


Students age: 9 - 17 years old
Minimum booking period is 1 week
Maximum booking period is 12 weeks
Ward 24/7
Homestay or hostel accommodation (meals included)
